About Us

Welcome to the Digital Recipe Book for Family & Friends, a dedicated space for preserving and sharing the sweetness of India's rich culinary traditions. This platform focuses exclusively on Indian sweets recipes, allowing family and friends to come together, contribute, and celebrate the joy of traditional desserts.

Services

  1. User Registration & Login: Secure registration and login system for users to create their own accounts and access recipes.
  2. Recipe Management: Users can add their own recipes, view recipes shared by others, and delete only the recipes they have created.
  3. Categorization & Filters: Recipes are organized and can be filtered by Indian States and Festivals for easy discovery.
  4. Recipe Sharing & Discovery: Encourages family and friends to share unique Indian sweets recipes and explore those contributed by others.
  5. Admin Control: The superuser (admin) has complete control over users, recipes, and categories to maintain the platform.
  6. Easy Access Anywhere: A web-based platform accessible on any device, making it easy to browse and share recipes from anywhere.
